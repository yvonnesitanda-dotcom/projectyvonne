B2B Offline Version

In our commitment for fair and wide distribution of educational resources around the globe, we aim to introduce Edmodo Classroom as offline package. For deployment and use in remote areas with unstable internet infrastructure, global teachers could simply access the offline version and access Edmodo Classroom’s abundant tools and resources, imparting knowledge with fun to their students




Immersive Classroom

With 101 VR Immersive Classroom solution, futuristic VR technology is being introduced into education. With top-quality educational resources at its core, it sets up a highly open, interactive and immersive 3D learning environment for today’s learners by combining terminals, application systems, platforms and contents.





Smart Classroom

With strong focus on cultivating student’s innovative spirit and knowledge points, Smart Classroom innovatively integrates Virtual Reality technology (VR) with STEM and MAKER education. Based on 101 VR Resource Editor, having plentiful 3D educational resources in its Cloud library, it provides students platform for visualization and transforming them into creators.
